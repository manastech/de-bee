What's this for?
Say you go to lunch with your friends. It's easier if someone pays for the rest of the group and then uploads the debts into De-Bee. The one that pays simply enters below who payed, what consumed each one, how much did they pay, and De-Bee will handle the rest.
  Example:
Pays: SomeMember
AnotherMember: stuff $8
MemberOne, MemberTwo: something they both bought $12
Buyer: one thing, another thing, and one more $20
